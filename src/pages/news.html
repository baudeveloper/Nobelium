    Nobelium Tech Corp. completes initial public offering

    HALIFAX, April 13, 2016 /CNW/ - NOBELIUM TECH CORP. ("Nobelium" or the "Company"), a capital pool company, is pleased to announce that it has completed its initial public offering (the "Offering") of 3,500,000 common shares at a price of $0.10 per common share for gross proceeds to the Company of $350,000. Richardson GMP Limited (the "Agent") acted as agent for the Offering and was paid a cash commission of $35,000, equal to 10% of the gross proceeds and granted a non-transferrable option ("Agent's Option") to purchase 350,000 common shares of the Company equal to 10% of the number of common shares sold through the Offering. The Agent's Option is exercisable for a period of 24 months at a price of $0.10 per common share
