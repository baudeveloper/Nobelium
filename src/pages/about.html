About Nobelium Tech Corp.

Company

Nobelium is a corporation that will own and acquire a growing collection of high quality, profitable SaaS businesses or platform businesses with recurring revenue models solving specific vertical or industry needs.

Nobelium will become a permanent home for these high-quality software companies, while enabling their entrepreneurs to keep running their businesses. Nobelium will provide them with capital and management expertise to facilitate future growth, as well as realizing cost savings via centralized finance and administration.

Corporate Mission and Strategy

We seek to compound shareholder capital at attractive per share rates over the long-term by prudently acquiring and building a collection of high-quality vertical market software (“VMS”) businesses and continuously reinvesting their cash earnings.

We believe that applying a disciplined capital allocation model to the VMS industry creates opportunity for above average shareholder returns.

Growing our invested capital per share by acquiring new companies and reinvesting in them, and adhering to a disciplined IRR and cash payback model on deployed capital is our core focus.

Nobelium aims to grow annual acquisition capital deployed by at least 25% per year.

In so doing, Nobelium seeks to…

  • provide a permanent home for high-quality VMS businesses
  • become an acquirer of choice status in the VMS industry
  • own a predictable and growing set of cash flows from operating businesses
  • leverage earnings shields; non-cash amortization and legacy tax losses

…which it believes are attractive and sustainable competitive advantages.

Management and Board of Directors

John Varghese, President and CEO, Chairman, Director

John Varghese is a senior professional with extensive investing, capital markets and operating experience having served on over 20 Board of Directors of public and private companies, industry associations and not for profit organizations. Currently, he is Chairman of Francium Corp., Chairman and CEO of Nobelium Tech Corp. and a member of the Advisory Board of Wellington Financial LP. Previously, he was Executive Chairman of Sprott Power Corp. (“SPZ.TO”). Other past Chairman roles include: Ventus Energy Inc. and Orion Securities Inc. (formerly Yorkton Securities). Mr. Varghese has held CEO, COO and CFO positions at start-up organizations, as well as senior management roles within CI Financial Corp., Royal Bank Capital Corporation, Merrill Lynch Canada, Dell Computer Corporation and Jim Pattison Industries Ltd.

Michael Anaka, CFO

Michael Anaka is a chartered accountant and is currently a Partner with PricewaterhouseCoopers in Halifax, NS. Mr. Anaka has experience sourcing, negotiating and arranging financing packages for clients in a variety of industries. He has served as the audit engagement partner for many significant Atlantic businesses.

Glenn Jessome, Corporate Secretary

Mr. Jessome is a securities and corporate lawyer practicing in Halifax, Nova Scotia, with extensive capital markets and technology transaction experience. Mr. Jessome received his law and degree and MBA from Dalhousie University in 1991 and his Bachelor of Science from St. Frances Xavier University in 1986. Mr. Jessome is a member of the National Advisory Committee for the TSX Venture Exchange and of the Quebec-Atlantic Canada Advisory Committee for the TSX Venture Exchange, and a member of the Institute of Corporate Directors and the Canadian Society of Corporate Secretaries.

Glen Lavigne, Director

Glen Lavigne is the Chief Executive Officer and President of SolutionInc Technologies Ltd, and a Director since 2003. He is a Business Development Professional with 17 years of international business experience. Mr. Lavigne was a Founding Member of the Visitor Based Networking Division of 3COM Corporation, and served as Regional Sales Manager from 1997 to 1999. He served as a Director of New Business Ventures at 3COM Corporation from 1999 to 2002.

Errol Treslan, Director

Erroll G. Treslan is a practicing lawyer based in Owen Sound, Ontario, practicing with Kirby Robinson Treslan Professional Corporation since 2002. Erroll is a graduate of Dalhousie Law School. Erroll is a member of the Law Society of Upper Canada and a Trustee of the Tom Thomson Art Foundation.
